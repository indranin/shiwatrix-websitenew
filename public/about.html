About Us

A Team, obsessed with scientific rigor.

Who We Are

A specialized scientific & regulatory consulting partner.

Shiwatrix Lifesciences Pvt Ltd is a specialized scientific and regulatory consulting firm supporting the medical device and pharmaceutical industries with evidence-based solutions and internationally accredited expertise. Scientific excellence and regulatory precision sit at the core of everything we do.

Our internationally accredited toxicologists and regulatory specialists bring over 20 years of professional experience, helping clients navigate complex regulatory requirements with confidence.

Our Story

Our original group of accredited toxicologists expanded into a full multidisciplinary team, and our regulatory reach broadened along with it — from an early focus on ISO 10993 biological evaluations to today's coverage of FDA, European MDR, UK, CDSCO, and FASC requirements across emerging global markets. Along the way we built out a complete service offering — Biological Evaluation Plans and Reports, Chemical Characterization, and Toxicological Risk Assessments — delivered through a disciplined process of consulting, assessing, documenting, supporting, and responding to every challenge a submission raises. With 250+ projects delivered for 80+ clients worldwide and a team anchored by ERT and DABT-certified toxicologists, we continue investing in the accredited expertise it takes to help healthcare innovations reach patients safely, faster, and in full compliance with international standards.

Our Vision

To be a trusted global leader in scientific and regulatory consulting, enabling healthcare innovations to reach the market safely and in compliance with international standards.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, science-based solutions that support product safety, regulatory excellence, and business success through quality, integrity, and innovation.

Our Expertise

Depth and breadth across the regulatory landscape.

Our team's strength lies in the depth and breadth of its scientific and regulatory experience.
